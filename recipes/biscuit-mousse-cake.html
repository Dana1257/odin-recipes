Biscuit Cream Cake

Description

This cake is extremely delicious. After having one piece I can't wait to devour another. The cake will keep in the fridge up to two weeks. On the counter for about two days. Either way you'll want to wrap the cake in parchment paper and then cling film to properly store it.

Ingredients

  • 500 milligrams whipping cream
  • 2 boxes of vanilla pudding powder
  • 3 packs of tea biscuits
  • 100 grams of dark chocolate
  • Strawberry Sauce
  • Strawberries/cut each one in half
  • Chocolate Syrup
  • Coffee for mixing

Steps

  1. Mix coffee and hot water
  2. Prepare the English cake pan
  3. Dip each biscuit (one at a time) for 1 second in the coffee mix and set them in the pan
  4. Spread chocolate syrup on top of the first layer of biscuits and make a second layer.
  5. Mix the whipping cream with the powder pudding and mix until it is hardened and spread on top of the first 2 layers.
  6. Repeat steps 3 and 4.
  7. Apply strawberry in the middle of the pan and on top of the strawberry, pour strawberry sauce.
  8. Take the rest of the whipping cream and apply on top of the cake.
  9. Put in the refrigerator until it settles.
  10. Melt the dark chocolate in the microwave let it sit for a little bit and pour it on top of the cake and put the cake in the refrigerator
