Marble Cake

Description

Marble cake is made when you add a small amount of dark-colored batter to light-colored batter and lightly mix it to give the cake a marbled appearance.

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1 ½ cup of sugar
  • ½ cup of oil
  • 1 cup of orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder
  • 2 cups of self-rising flour

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 160 degrees℃.
  2. Separate the egg whites from the yolks.
  3. Using a mixer whip the egg whites with half a cup of sugar.
  4. In a different bowl whip the yolk with one cup of sugar until the yolk becomes lighter.
  5. Add the oil, the orange juice, vanilla sugar, the baking powder, and the flour. Mix it all until it becomes smooth.
  6. Add the whipped egg whites to the yolk in a gradual manner.
  7. Pour into a baking pan and leave a quarter of the mix.
  8. Add the one tablespoon cocoa powder to the quarter mix. Mix it well and pour on top of the mix in the pan.
  9. Use the opposite side of the wooden spoon and dip in the cake and make the design of your choice.
  10. Bake for 55 minutes at 160 degrees℃.
